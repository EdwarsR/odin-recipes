Where do arepas come from?

Arepas are a traditional food typical Venezuela and Colombia. They're stuffed cornmeal cakes that are crispy and fluffy

Ingredients

  • Pre-cooked white corn flour
  • Cold water
  • Salt teaspoon dessert
  • Crallado cheese for the filling
  • Colded chicken breast for the filling
  • Small avocado for the filling

Steps

  1. mix corn flour, water and salt
  2. we make six equal balls in size
  3. work them with our hands and flatten them delicately
  4. we cook them in a nonstick pan, with olive oil about six minutes
  5. we make a cut on one side and fill it to taste with the mixture of ingredients: cheese, chicken and avocado crushed with a fork
